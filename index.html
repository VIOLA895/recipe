CHICKEN STEW RECIPE

A delicious and flavorful chicken stew recipe

Chicken stew is beloved for its comforting warmth and ease of preparation. This hearty dish combines tender chicken, vegetables, and sometimes potatoes in a flavorful broth or sauce. Its versatility allows for personalization with various herbs and spices, while its nutritional value, providing protein and vitamins from vegetables, adds to its appeal. Chicken stew's rich, savory broth is a hallmark, enhancing its comforting qualities. It's a favorite for cold weather or when seeking a satisfying, wholesome meal that brings together flavors in a single pot, making it both convenient and enjoyable for a wide range of tastes.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 full chicken(cut into pieces)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1/2-1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 2-3 grated cloves of garlic
  • 1 fresh green/red chili(optional)
  • 4 tomatoes (washed and chopped)
  • 1 large capsicum (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 chicken cube(optional)
  • salt(use sparingly if using a chicken cube)
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • handful of dhania
